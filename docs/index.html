About Me

I am Lohit Marla, a dedicated Data Engineer and aspiring Data Scientist with a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of Connecticut and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from GITAM University. My professional journey spans over four years at Novus Platform, where I excelled in designing and optimizing complex data pipelines, implementing real-time analytics, and enhancing data governance for large-scale systems. I also bring valuable experience as a Data Engineer Intern at HexStream, where I developed machine learning models and interactive dashboards to improve forecasting accuracy, and as a Data Engineer at Parker Dewey, where I automated ML pipelines and architected data solutions for seamless integration. My expertise lies in data engineering, machine learning, and data analysis, with hands-on experience in Python, R, SQL, Airflow, AWS, and Tableau. Throughout my career, I have successfully led impactful projects, such as forecasting freight indices, building real-time object detection workflows, and optimizing healthcare data pipelines, achieving significant improvements in data accuracy, decision-making, and operational efficiency. Passionate about transforming data into actionable insights, I aim to drive innovation and deliver solutions that enable organizations to thrive in a data-driven world.
